Our Services

Full-Service Plumbing in South Bend & Surrounding Communities

At Goff Heating & Plumbing, we've been delivering expert plumbing solutions to the South Bend area for over 85 years. Our certified plumbers combine deep local knowledge with modern techniques — so every job is handled right, on time, and within budget.

Water Heater Repair & Installation

Fast diagnosis and repair of tank and tankless water heaters. When replacement is needed, we'll help you choose and install the right system for your home or business.

Leak Detection & Pipe Repair

Hidden leaks can cause serious damage before they're visible. Our plumbers use modern detection methods to find and fix leaks fast — protecting your home and your wallet.

Drain Cleaning & Clog Removal

Slow drains and stubborn clogs cleared at the source. We handle kitchen drains, bathroom drains, main lines, and sewer backups — no temporary fixes, lasting results.

Bathroom Plumbing

Toilet repairs and replacements, faucet installs, showerhead upgrades, tub and shower work — everything your bathroom needs done right the first time.

Kitchen Plumbing

Sink and faucet installs, garbage disposal repairs, dishwasher connections, and under-sink pipe work. Keep your kitchen running smoothly with South Bend area's trusted plumbers.

New Construction & Remodel Plumbing
